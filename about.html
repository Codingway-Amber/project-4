About Me

I’ve been drawing digitally since I was 13 years old when I got my first Wacom tablet. Digital Art is my preferred method of drawing and creating art. I’ve always drawn in an anime art style because I was a huge Otaku (anime nerd) my whole childhood and watched YouTube art tutorials from artists who had a similar style, such as Mark Crilley and Sophie-Chan. Today, I mainly find inspiration for drawing through video games and specifically Eastern-made games like JRPGs. The character designs are always so beautiful that I can’t help wanting to draw them! I plan to go into game development as a career. While I aim to work in large studios and companies to start, I intend to break off and become an indie game developer eventually. It’s my biggest dream to draw and design characters and assets as well as write a captivating story for my own game where I can make the main creative decisions through the whole process. To that end, I’ve started to learn programming/ coding to ensure I’m well-rounded.
